How to Delete Your Spectramentis Account

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your account from the Spectramentis app.

  1. Open the Spectramentis app. You will first see the Landing Page of the application.
  2. From the landing page, open the Left Sidebar and go to the Profile section.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile section and tap the Account Delete button.
    This button is located at the very bottom of your profile.
  4. A confirmation modal will appear. Enter your password and tap the Delete button to permanently delete your account.
    This action is irreversible.
