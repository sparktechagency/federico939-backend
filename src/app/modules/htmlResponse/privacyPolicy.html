MamaCare — Privacy Policy

MamaCare is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile app and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email, profile photo (optional), address/location (for booking purposes).
  • Booking Details: Date, time, and location of bookings, number of children, and any notes you provide.
  • Payment Information: Payment method (processed securely via trusted third-party providers; we do not store your card details).
  • Device & App Usage: Device type, IP address, location (only when booking/tracking is active), usage patterns, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Match parents with nearby available nannies.
  • Manage bookings, availability, and notifications.
  • Process secure payments and provide receipts.
  • Improve app performance, support, and user experience.
  • Send essential updates, alerts, and optional promotional messages.

3. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with:

  • Trusted payment processors (e.g., Paystack, Stripe)
  • Customer support and identity/verification services
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Your Privacy Choices

  • Edit your profile anytime
  • Enable or disable location permissions
  • Turn off marketing emails
  • Request account deletion by contacting support

5. Data Security

We use encryption, firewalls, and secure servers to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device protected and use strong passwords.

6. Children’s Privacy

MamaCare is not intended for users under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Major updates will be communicated through email or in-app notifications.
